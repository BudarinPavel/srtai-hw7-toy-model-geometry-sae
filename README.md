# Homework 7 — Studying the Geometry of a Toy Model

This repository contains my solution for **Homework 7** of the master's course **Selected Research Topics in AI**.

## Topic

The project studies the geometry of a **Toy Model of superposition** and the behavior of **Sparse Autoencoders (SAEs)** trained on its hidden states.

The main goal is to analyze how changes in the toy model and training setup affect:

- the geometry of feature embeddings and hidden states,
- the reconstruction quality of SAE,
- the recovery of underlying feature frequencies and embeddings.

## Homework scope

The assignment includes the following main parts:

1. **Geometry of the toy model**  
   Study how model parameters affect the structure and geometry of feature embeddings and hidden states.

2. **SAE quality analysis**  
   Analyze what influences SAE quality, measured for example by explained variance.

3. **Recovery of true features**  
   Study how well SAE recovers the original feature frequencies and embeddings.

4. **Optional open-ended research part**  
   Formulate and test additional hypotheses related to the setup.
